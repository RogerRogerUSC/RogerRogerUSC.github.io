Bio

Zhe is currently a first-year Ph.D. student in Computing and Information Sciences program at Golisano College of Computing and Information Sciences at Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT), advised by Dr. Haibo Yang. His research interests mainly concentrate on addressing issues caused by heterogeneity and improving communication and memory efficiency of federated or large-scale distributed systems by optimization techniques (e.g., first-order and zeroth-order optimization).

I am glad to discuss ideas about my research interests, so please feel free to reach out!
