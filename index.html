Bio

Zhe is currently a second-year Ph.D. student in Computing and Information Sciences program at Golisano College of Computing and Information Sciences at Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT), advised by Dr. Haibo Yang. He applies tools from optimization theory in the following fields:

  • Large-Scale Distributed/Federated Learning: Tackling system and statistical heterogeneity, enhancing communication efficiency, and accelerating convergence in collaborative training.

  • Efficient Training of Large Language Models (LLMs): Designing memory-, communication-, and parameter-efficient algorithms and frameworks for scalable fine-tuning and pretraining.

I am glad to discuss ideas about my research interests, so please feel free to reach out!
